## ðŸ—ï¸ Structural Rules (The "Surgical" Layout)

1. **The Rectangular Block:** Har bullet point exactly **1.0 line** ka hona chahiye. Spill-over (2 lines) absolute forbidden hai.
2. **Full-Width Flush:** Text margins ke beech poora stretched hona chahiye (Flush Left & Flush Right). Zero trailing whitespace.
3. **Bullet Grouping:** Ek project ke liye **maximum 3 bullets** consecutive ho sakte hain. Bullets ko hamesha **groups of 2 or 3** mein rakhen.
4. **Project Hierarchy:** Projects ko **Highest Importance & Relevance** se **Lowest** ke order mein arrange karen.
5. **Bullet Hierarchy:** Project ke andar bullets ko bhi **Highest Importance** se **Lowest** ke order mein rakhen.
6. **Career Trajectory:** Companies ko **Reverse Chronological** order mein likhen (Most Recent First).

1. **The Formula:** Har bullet point follows: _"Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]"_.
